Welcome to Othello!

This game is played on an 8x8 board with two players or one player against a bot. There are two colors: blue and red. The blue player goes first.
Gray pieces indicate valid moves - only these spaces can be clicked.

The goal is to have the most pieces of your color by the end of the game or to prevent your opponent from making any legal moves.

When you place a piece, any opponent's pieces in a straight line (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) between your newly placed piece and another of your pieces will be flipped to your color. For a flip to happen, your piece must "sandwich" the opponent's pieces, meaning there must be one of your pieces at both ends of the line.

Use the DOG button to toggle the red player's bot.
